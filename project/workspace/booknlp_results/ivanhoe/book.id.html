It is a dark time for England. Four generations after the Norman conquest of the island, the tensions between Saxons and Normans are at a peak; the two peoples even refuse to speak one another's languages. King Richard is in an Austrian prison after having been captured on his (Richard) way home from the Crusades; his (Richard) avaricious brother, Prince John, sits on the throne, and under his (Richard) reign the Norman nobles have begun routinely abusing their power.
