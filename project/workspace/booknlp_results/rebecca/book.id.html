Rebecca's narrative takes the form of a flashback. The heroine, who remains nameless, lives in Europe with her (heroine) husband, Maxim de Winter, traveling from hotel to hotel, harboring memories of a beautiful home called Manderley, which, we learn, has been destroyed by fire.
