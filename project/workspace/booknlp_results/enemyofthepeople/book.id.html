He (Stockmann) alerts several members of the community, including Hovstad and Aslaksen, and receives generous support and thanks for making his (Stockmann) discovery in time to save the town. Dr. Stockmann then begins a long tirade in which he (mayor) condemns the foundations of the town and the tyranny of the majority. The audience finds his (mayor) speech incredibly offensive, and the next morning the doctor's home is vandalized. He (mayor) and his (mayor) daughter are fired. The mayor insinuates that the doctor's actions were merely a scheme to inherit more of Morten Kiil's money, and Kiil himself soon arrives to suggest just such a plan to Dr. Stockmann. However, the doctor refuses all such suggestions and decides to defy authority and remain in town. His (doctor) family is supportive, and he (doctor) says that the strongest man is the man who stands alone.
